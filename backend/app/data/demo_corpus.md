## About Acme CloudSync

Acme CloudSync is a file synchronization service that keeps folders in sync
across multiple devices. It supports Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, and iOS.
Files are encrypted in transit and at rest. The service is operated by Acme
Corp and is intended for both individuals and small teams.

## Plans and Pricing

CloudSync offers three plans. The Free plan includes 5 GB of storage and syncs
up to two devices. The Personal plan costs 8 dollars per month and includes
500 GB of storage with unlimited devices. The Team plan costs 15 dollars per
user per month and includes 2 TB of pooled storage, shared team folders, and
priority support. All paid plans are billed monthly and can be cancelled at any
time from the account settings page.

## Getting Started

To get started, create an account at the CloudSync website, then download the
desktop app for your operating system. After installing, sign in and choose the
folders you want to sync. The first sync may take a while depending on how much
data you have. A green checkmark on a file means it has finished syncing.

## Storage and File Limits

The maximum size for a single file is 50 GB on paid plans and 2 GB on the Free
plan. There is no limit on the number of files beyond your total storage quota.
Deleted files are kept in the trash for 30 days on paid plans and 7 days on the
Free plan, after which they are permanently removed.

## Sharing Files

You can share any file or folder by right clicking it in the desktop app and
choosing Share. CloudSync creates a secure link that you can send to anyone.
Links can be password protected and given an expiry date. Team plan members can
also share folders directly with other members of their team for live
collaboration.

## Security and Privacy

All data is encrypted using AES 256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Two factor
authentication is available and strongly recommended for all accounts. Acme
Corp does not sell user data. Backups are retained for 90 days. If you suspect
your account has been compromised, change your password immediately and contact
support.

## Troubleshooting Sync Issues

If a file is not syncing, first check that the desktop app is running and signed
in. Make sure you have not exceeded your storage quota. Files with names that
contain characters not allowed by your operating system will not sync. Pausing
and resuming sync from the app menu resolves most temporary issues. If problems
persist, view the sync log from the app settings.

## Cancelling or Changing a Plan

You can change or cancel your plan at any time from the Billing section of your
account settings. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing
period. If your storage use is above the limit of the plan you downgrade to,
syncing of new files pauses until you free up space, but existing files remain
available for download.

## Contact and Support

Free plan users receive support through the community forum and help center.
Personal and Team plan users receive email support, and Team plan users receive
priority responses within one business day. For urgent account or security
issues, use the contact form in the help center.
